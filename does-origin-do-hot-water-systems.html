Troubleshooting common issues with Origin hot water systems

If you’re experiencing issues with your Origin hot water system, there are some common troubleshooting steps you can take before considering a Hot Water System Replacement. One prevalent issue is inadequate hot water, which might be a result of a faulty thermostat or a buildup of sediment in the tank. In such cases, adjusting the thermostat settings or flushing the tank could potentially resolve the problem.

Another issue that homeowners may encounter is discoloured or odorous water coming from their Origin hot water system. This could signal rust in the pipes or tank, or a bacterial growth issue. To address this, flushing the system with a cleaning solution recommended by Origin and ensuring proper maintenance of the unit can often eliminate these concerns. Always remember that in case of persistent or major issues, consulting with a professional for a Hot Water System Replacement might be the most effective solution.
